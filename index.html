Anant Shah's Portfolio

Producer/Audio Engineer/Composer

Anant Shah is a music producer, audio engineer, and composer, currently based out of Los Angeles. His work generally includes producing beats and working on mixes for a variety of artists, but with a focus on Hip-Hop and RnB. In his work, he strives to combine sounds of the past (sampling, lofi fx, etc) with modern production practices, creating a sound that is simultaneosly vintage and futuristic. He has worked with artists from around the globe such as Jlav (New York) and Sally Han (South Korea).

He is currently pursuing a BFA in music technology from the California Institute of the Arts.
